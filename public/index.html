How It Works

Three simple steps to recover your belongings

1

Report Found Items

Found something on campus? Sign in and fill out a quick form with details about the item, where you found it, and optionally upload a photo.

2

Search & Browse

Lost something? Search our database by keyword or category to see if anyone has turned in your item. No account needed to search.

3

Submit a Claim

Found your item listed? Submit a claim with proof of ownership. The admin team will review it and help coordinate the return.
